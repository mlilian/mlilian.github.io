Academic background

Before taking up an assistant professorship in Hanover in April 2015, I held postdoc positions at the Institut de Mathématiques de Jussieu, at l'Université Paris-Sud (Orsay) and at the University of Bristol. I completed my PhD in combinatorial number theory under the supervision of Ben Green at the University of Cambridge in 2011.
